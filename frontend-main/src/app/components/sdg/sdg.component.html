No poverty

The No Poverty SDG (Sustainable Development Goal) aims to eradicate extreme poverty by ensuring equal access to basic resources and services, including education, healthcare, and social protection. It also focuses on creating sustainable livelihoods and economic opportunities for people living in poverty, particularly women and marginalized groups.
